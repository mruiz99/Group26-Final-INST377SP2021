About the Data

Source: Video Game Dataset

This dataset is a representation of video games and their attributes ranging from 1969 to 2021. Due to the lack of records from the 60s to 80s, our analysis does not include these years. The same can be said for the year 2021 as there have not been many games added to the dataset from this year. The dataset includes a games title, release date, genre, respectively console, developer, publisher, and some user/critic ratings. For this analysis, we utilized the genre and release date of each game from 1990-2020.

About this webpage

Our group is passionate about video games and this website is designed to display the popularity of various genres over a span of three decades. Our purpose is to provide visitors with information on the history of video game sales through graphic displays between the regions of North America and Japan.
